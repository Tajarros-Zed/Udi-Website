Top 5 Destination of Ilocos Region

Bonuan Blue Beach in Ilocos Region

Bonuan Blue Beach or commonly known as Tondaligan Beach is located at Bonuan Tondaligan, Dagupan City. It is one of the most popular beaches here in Pangasinan. Most of us already heard some negative comments about this beach. But hey! The beach has been rehabilitated and now it is different from the Bonuan Blue Beach that is on our mind. This place is a good place for picnics and relaxation. It is clean and well-organized. There is a children's park that kids will surely love. It's just a minutes away from the city proper.
