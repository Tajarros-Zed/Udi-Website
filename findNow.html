10 Best Destination in Philippines

Discover the Most Popular Destination in the Philippines

In the Philippines, a nation of more than 7,000 islands, there are numerous scenic beaches. These breathtaking beaches may be found around the country. Some places are well-known for their fine sand and crystal-clear water, while others are popular for their exciting nightlife.

Among our top favorite beaches, there are probably a few that you may have never heard. This list of the top beaches in the Philippines may inspire some fresh travel plans if you have a thirst for adventure. The distance between each beach may seem far, but the trip is definitely worth the effort!

Top 10 Destination in the Philippines

Bonuan Blue Beach in Ilocos Region

Bonuan Blue Beach or commonly known as Tondaligan Beach is located at Bonuan Tondaligan, Dagupan City. It is one of the most popular beaches here in Pangasinan. Most of us already heard some negative comments about this beach. But hey! The beach has been rehabilitated and now it is different from the Bonuan Blue Beach that is on our mind. This place is a good place for picnics and relaxation. It is clean and well-organized. There is a children's park that kids will surely love. It's just a minutes away from the city proper.
